Basic Financial Calculator

A collection of free tools for Thai households and professionals to assess loans, deposits, and time value scenarios with localized assumptions.

Available Calculators

  • Loan repayment schedule calculator
  • Future value projection
  • Net present value evaluator
  • Savings Actual/365 interest calculator
  • Fixed deposit comparison
  • Tiered deposit ladder

Why this platform

The calculators follow Bank of Thailand conventions, support Thai and English interfaces, and export schedules in CSV or Excel formats for reporting.
